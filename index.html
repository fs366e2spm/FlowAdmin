Power Automate Flow Admin

This tool provides a streamlined way to manage your Power Automate flows. Easily find specific flows, group them, and review their current status and run history at a glance, helping you quickly and efficiently oversee your automation workflows.

Privacy Notice: This application uses Microsoft secure authentication to sign in and access your Power Automate flows.

All authentication and data requests are handled directly between you and Microsoft, ensuring your information remains secure and private. This page does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.

It simply provides a convenient interface to display and manage your flow data.
